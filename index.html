What is a Rodder?

We are a community of gamers, creators, designers, developers and nerds. We love playing games, creating things and sharing stuff. By working together we can reach more people and help share our talents with the world.

Want to join the Team?

Awesome! All you have to do is apply to our Google group. Make sure to include a link to your content (youtube, github, tumblr, instagram, deviant art, twitch, whatever) in the application request so we can add you to the list. Just FYI, we will use your google avatar as the link in the Rodders section unless you tell us otherwise.

If you want your content to go in the Feed section, you'll need to send us the exact link of the content you want to be fed out. This should be something that you update regularly.
