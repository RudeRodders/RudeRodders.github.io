What is a Rodder?

We are a community of gamers, creators, designers, developers and nerds. We love playing games, creating things and sharing stuff. We know working together we can reach more people and help share our talents with the world.

Want to join the Team?

Awesome! Send us a tweet, drop us an email, or message us on Facebook. Make sure to include a link to your content (youtube, website, github, deviant art, whatever) and we'll get you started.
