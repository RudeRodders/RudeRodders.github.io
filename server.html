Bukkit World List
* * * Adventurer * * *
This is the dungeon world. There are lots of monsters, crazy dungeons, traps and all sorts of fun ways to die. Now with Cake Defense.

* * * Builder * * *
This world is purely creative. Monsters do not spawn here. You have free range to build/mine where ever you want as long as it does not interfere with other player's creations/mines. This is where you should build if you want to be promoted in the server. Make it EPIC.

* * * Eldar * * *
This world is a massive adventure map with a storyline written by our own gladiator1080.

* * * Gamer * * *
In this world you can play great games like Blockey, Darts, Clue, Monopoly, Yog Golf and the Yog-Olympics... Game ON.

* * * Hunger * * *
The infamous Hunger Games map where players go head to head to see who can be the last one standing...

* * * Midgar * * *
This world is a replica of the city from Final Fantasy XII... it also serves as spawn and gateway to all the other worlds... Don't forget to check out the library.

* * * Skyblocker * * *
This world is a skyblock map where you must complete challenges with very limited resources.

* * * Tester * * *
This is a flat-world where we test out new mods, builds, maps, etc... watch out for the slimes.
